# Company Glossary

- **Guide** — An independent outdoor instructor or activity leader who lists bookable experiences on Terrain. Guides are the supply side of the marketplace. They pay no listing fee; Terrain takes 18% of each completed booking.

- **Adventurer** — A user who discovers and books outdoor experiences on Terrain. Adventurers are the demand side. They pay the guide's listed price plus a 3% Terrain platform fee at checkout.

- **Experience** — A single bookable outdoor activity listed by a guide. Examples: "2-hour surf lesson at Ocean Beach," "Full-day alpine climb on Mt. Rainier," "Foraging walk in Muir Woods." Each experience has a listed price, capacity, availability calendar, and booking mode (Request to Book or Instant Book).

- **Instant Book** — A booking mode where adventurers can confirm a booking without waiting for guide approval. The experience is automatically confirmed upon payment. Currently in beta with 15% of guides; 22% of eligible guides have opted in. Detail-to-booking conversion on Instant Book listings is about 32% higher than Request to Book listings.

- **Request to Book** — The default booking mode. Adventurers request a booking and the guide has 24 hours to accept or decline. Median acceptance time is 18 hours.

- **Guide Pro** — Terrain's optional paid subscription for guides, at $29/month. Includes: analytics dashboard (listing views, conversion, revenue trends), priority placement in search results, and bulk availability management. Currently 340 active subscribers (~14% of active guides).

- **Listing activation rate** — The percentage of guides who sign up and publish at least one experience within 30 days of registration. Currently 62%; target is 75%.

- **GMV (Gross Merchandise Value)** — Total dollar value of bookings processed through Terrain before the take rate is applied. Current annualized GMV: $18M. The primary top-line marketplace metric.

- **Take rate** — Terrain's 18% commission on each completed booking. Applied to the guide's listed price. Take-rate revenue = GMV × 18%. This excludes the 3% adventurer platform fee and Guide Pro subscription revenue.

- **Platform fee** — The 3% fee charged to adventurers at checkout, on top of the guide's listed price. Separate from the take rate; both are Terrain revenue.

- **Booking conversion** — Bookings completed as a percentage of search sessions. Currently 2.8% overall. Related funnel cuts should name their denominator explicitly; for example, Instant Book-eligible listings show about 32% higher detail-to-booking conversion than Request to Book listings.

- **Guide activation** — The milestone event when a newly registered guide publishes their first experience. Guides who don't activate within 30 days churn at much higher rates. Currently 38% of registered guides never activate.

- **Adventurer repeat rate** — Percentage of adventurers who complete 2 or more bookings within a 12-month window. Currently 38%. A key retention metric and the primary gap in the current product (no structured re-engagement loop exists).

- **Cancellation rate** — Percentage of bookings cancelled by either party before the experience date. Currently 14%; industry benchmark is 7–9%. Split approximately 60% adventurer-initiated, 40% guide-initiated.

- **Squad** — Terrain's name for a cross-functional product team. Two squads: Adventurer Experience (search, booking, reviews, notifications) and Guide Experience (onboarding, listing, availability, payouts). Both squads report to the VP of Product.

- **TRN-XXXX** — Terrain's Linear ticket ID format (e.g., TRN-4821). Used in all project management references.
