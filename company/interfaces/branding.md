# Branding

## Color Palette

- **Primary (Forest Green):** `#2D6A4F` — brand dominant; used for primary buttons, active navigation, key UI elements
- **Secondary (Mid Green):** `#40916C` — accent and hover states; used for secondary actions and highlights
- **Accent (Amber):** `#F9A825` — CTAs, badges, and attention-grabbing UI elements; used sparingly
- **Background (Warm Off-White):** `#FAFAF7` — page backgrounds; feels natural, not sterile
- **Surface (White):** `#FFFFFF` — card backgrounds, modals, input fields
- **Text (Near-Black):** `#1A1A1A` — primary body text
- **Secondary Text:** `#6B6B6B` — metadata, captions, secondary labels
- **Dividers:** `#E5E5E5` — borders, rules, subtle separators
- **Success:** `#2D6A4F` (same as primary green)
- **Warning:** `#F9A825` (same as accent amber)
- **Error:** `#C0392B`

---

## Typography

- **Heading font:** DM Sans Bold — used for H1, H2, slide titles, display text
- **Body font:** DM Sans Regular — used for all body copy, labels, UI text
- **Monospace font:** JetBrains Mono — used for code snippets, event names, data display
- **Font weights in use:** Regular (400), Medium (500), Bold (700)
- **Type scale (approximate):**
  - Display / H1: 32px / Bold
  - H2: 24px / Bold
  - H3: 18px / Medium
  - Body: 16px / Regular
  - Small / caption: 13px / Regular

---

## Logo

- **Primary wordmark:** "Terrain" in DM Sans Bold, forest green (`#2D6A4F`), with a small mountain/ridge mark to the left of the wordmark
- **Tagline:** "Find your guide" — appears on marketing materials, not in product UI
- **Icon / mark:** Simplified ridge silhouette in green; used as app icon, favicon, and in contexts where the full wordmark doesn't fit
- **Light variant:** White wordmark + white icon — for use on dark or green backgrounds
- **Dark variant:** Forest green wordmark + icon — for use on white or light backgrounds
- **Clear space:** Padding equal to the height of the mountain mark on all sides
- **Minimum size:** Never smaller than 80px wide for the full wordmark; 24px for the icon mark alone

---

## Slide Defaults

For `presentation-deck` Slides mode, use the following defaults:

- **Background:** `#FAFAF7` (warm off-white) for content slides; `#2D6A4F` (forest green) for title slide and section dividers
- **Title slide:** Green background, white DM Sans Bold headline, white DM Sans Regular subtitle; Terrain wordmark (white variant) bottom-left
- **Content slides:** White/off-white background; `#1A1A1A` text; forest green for emphasis and section labels
- **Section divider slides:** Forest green background, white headline, no body text
- **Header / footer:** Terrain icon mark (color variant) bottom-left on content slides; page number bottom-right; no slide title in header
- **Content alignment:** Left-aligned text; 40px margin on left and right; 32px top/bottom
- **Charts and data viz:** Use forest green (`#2D6A4F`) as the primary series color; amber (`#F9A825`) for secondary or highlighted series; gray (`#6B6B6B`) for comparison/baseline

---

## Voice and Tone (for presentation copy)

- **Confident, not corporate.** Lead with the finding, not the caveat.
- **Specific, not generic.** "Booking conversion is 2.8%, down 0.3pts from last quarter" not "performance was mixed."
- **Outdoorsy but not clichéd.** Terrain's brand has personality — but avoid forced outdoors metaphors ("we're climbing toward our goal") that feel try-hard.
- **Short sentences, active voice.** Slide copy is not a prose essay.
